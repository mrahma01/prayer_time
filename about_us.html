About Us

The Edgware Islamic Cultural Trust (EICT) is a recently formed charity. Edgware Islamic Cultural Trust has been granted charity status by Charity Commission from 6th September 2010. Charity Registration number is 1137809. We are not associated or subsidiary of any other organisation or charity.  However we are open to work with all organisations with common objective.  Visit Charity Commission website for more details www.charitycommission.gov.uk

The Edgware Islamic Cultural Trust key focus is to provide services for the growing Muslim communities of Edgware and surrounding areas, drawing on our Islamic values, with a view to improving quality life of the needy and enhancing community cohesion in Edgware.

Edgware is home to several thousand Muslim families according to the 2001 census, the Muslim population of Edgware continues to grow and there is significant gap in meeting the needs of the Muslims in the area. There is no mosque or permanent Islamic community prayer facility available in Edgware or close surrounding.

Our objective is
1. to advance further the Islamic faith by providing for advice, information and prayer rooms used by Muslims of Edgware for worship according to the religion of islam.
2. to advance the education and training of the unemployed public in particular to the Muslim community of Edgware.

We are actively seeking a permanent place of worship and open to work with all muslim organisation or charity in the area with common objectives.

Our aims are to:
  • Focus on the needs of the Muslim community in Edgware according to the Quran, Sunnah and Hadith.
  • Provide Muslims and non-Muslims with the opportunity to learn and understand Islam, through dialogue, discussion and social interaction.
  • Meet the needs of the local community by promoting health, education and basic IT training to seek employment opportunities.
  • Contribute to the social, cultural, spiritual and economic enhancement of the community.
  • Work towards enhancing community cohesion, promoting tolerance and opposing extremism.

     

    We would strongly like to work with everyone regardless of their ethnic origin.
