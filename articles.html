Project Updates

15 April 2011

Edgware Islamic Cultural Trust have completed the lease and received keys for 82 Chandos Crescent, Edgware HA8 6HL (5 minutes away from Edgware Station) with D1 use.
Inshallah, we plan to utilise this as a prayer place, also to run Quran classes for children, Brothers and Sisters along with other educational/training activities.
We have started the renovation work; therefore your help, participation, feedback and donation will be greatly welcomed.
We plan to keep you updated regularly about this project through our website.
